About Us

Built on Sudanese Rose.

At the core of RozaRed is Sudanese rose — not an additional ingredient, but the main base of the entire blend. Its properties provide a high level of flavor delivery compared to traditional and alternative bases.

  • Low essential oil content — clean flavor, no harsh notes
  • Dense leaf structure — high heat resistance
  • Stable aroma throughout the entire session
  • 100% Tobacco-Free & Nicotine-Free

“Sudanese rose is not a marketing concept — it is the technological foundation that defines the product's quality.”
